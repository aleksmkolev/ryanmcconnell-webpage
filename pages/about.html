Professional qualifications, experience, and therapeutic philosophy

Therapeutic Specializations

My training encompasses a wide range of therapeutic modalities, allowing me to tailor treatment approaches to each individual client's needs. This multi-modal approach ensures that we can find the most effective combination of techniques for your specific situation.

Treatment Philosophy

My approach to therapy is rooted in the belief that mental health and physical wellbeing are intrinsically connected. I focus on creating sustainable, long-term changes rather than temporary fixes. The goal is to empower you with the tools and confidence needed to become your own therapist, capable of managing challenges independently.

I believe in treating the whole person, not just the symptoms. This holistic approach addresses the mind-body connection through evidence-based therapeutic techniques combined with lifestyle factors such as nutrition, physical fitness, and breathwork.

Location & Availability

I provide services both online and in-person in Doha, Qatar. This flexibility ensures that geographical constraints don't prevent you from accessing the support you need. Online sessions are conducted through secure, confidential platforms that protect your privacy while maintaining the effectiveness of face-to-face therapy.

Important Note: At no point do I prescribe any medication of any kind. My approach focuses purely on therapeutic and lifestyle interventions that promote natural healing and resilience.

Why Choose This Approach?

Evidence-Based

All therapeutic approaches used are scientifically accredited and supported by peer-reviewed evidence, ensuring you receive treatments with proven effectiveness.

Multi-Modal Integration

By combining multiple therapeutic techniques simultaneously, clients often experience significant improvement within just a few sessions rather than months of single-approach therapy.

Holistic Wellness

Treatment addresses mental health through the lens of overall wellbeing, incorporating physical health, nutrition, and lifestyle factors for comprehensive healing.

Self-Empowerment

The ultimate goal is to equip you with the skills and confidence to manage your mental health independently, reducing long-term dependency on therapy.
